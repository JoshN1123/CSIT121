Baked Mac and Cheese Recipe

Made by Nagi, page source name "Baked Mac and Cheese"

This is a reader-favourite recipe included by popular demand in my debut cookbook “Dinner”!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Cheese
  • Garlic onion mustard
  • Flour
  • Mozzarella
  • Milk
  • Panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta
  2. Toss in butter
  3. Mix together toppings. Set asaide
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Make roux
  6. Add milk
  7. Add Seasonings
  8. Thicken sauce
  9. Add cheese
  10. Check salt
  11. Assemble
  12. Bake
  13. Serve

This is my favorite recipe because I love baked mac and cheese in general, any kind is good to me!
